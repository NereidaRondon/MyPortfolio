About

Who am I?

I am a married mother of two living in sunny (and hot!) SW Florida with our tabby cat, Saber T. Tiger. Born and raised in Florida, my pronouns are she/her, and I am of latin descent and speak and fully understand Spanish. I have taught high school math for almost 11 years, to over 1500 learners.

Why Software Development?

Software Development encompasses many of the aspects of teaching I enjoyed most, planning out a process of how to complete a project, creating said product with technology, working out the kinks (bugs) to make it better, and delivering a viable product for users (other teachers and students).

How did I get here?

I realized my choices for advancement in public education were very limited. Any real promotion would take me out of the classroom, so I thought if I have to leave the classroom to grow professionally then I would expand my options outside of the public education field.

What interests me?

I most enjoy spending time with my family, going on family trips, and teaching my children about the world. On my free time, I enjoy reading science fiction novels with a preference for dystopias. My guilty pleasure is playing Call of Duty Warzone with my husband on my PC. I'm an avid movie watcher and love binge watching tv shows on long weekends. My favorite franchises are Marvel and Star Wars.
