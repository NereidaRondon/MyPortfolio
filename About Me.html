How did I get here?

For most of my life, teaching was the end game. I was great at it, I loved the maths, and I enjoyed working with and shaping young adults. At some point, however, education was no longer my retirement plan. With a raw potential and a desire for more, I started my career transition.

In my existential search for a new career path, I stumbled upon a new found ability in an old interest... coding. While building a portfolio website, for my now debunked plan of transitioning into Instructional Design and Development, I discovered a passion for web development. I have always been intrigued by the idea of software development but I never saw myself venturing into that world until I unexpectedly walked through the door.

As I continued to work on my website,I discovered that this process that I was doing... researching, learning about web development, and then making it happen on my computer screen... was what I much rather be doing as a career. Plans were readjusted. I figured out that Frontend development is where I want to be and started learning from every where and anywhere.
